**Effective from:** 15/05/2026 · **Version:** 1.0
**Developer:** Nexttech Smart Solutions Company Limited

Free Scan is a document scanning application that works fully offline on your device. This policy describes how we handle data when you use the app and our commitment to protecting your privacy.

---

## 1. Information we collect

Free Scan **does not require accounts**, **does not collect personal information**, and **does not upload your documents to any server**. All scanned images, OCR text, and app settings are stored directly on your device. We do not access, receive, or analyse this content in any form.

## 2. Purpose of on-device data processing

Data on your device is processed locally to: display and manage your scanned documents, export watermark-free PDF/JPG files, perform text recognition (OCR) via Google ML Kit running on-device, persist user settings (language, theme, save folder), and count successful scans to power the in-app rating prompt. No document content ever leaves your device.

## 3. Data security

Your documents are stored in the application's private storage (Android Internal Storage / iOS App Sandbox), isolated by the operating system from other apps. The only outbound network connections are to Google Play Services over TLS 1.3, used exclusively for in-app billing (Google Play Billing) and the rating dialog (Play In-App Review). No document, image, or OCR text is ever transmitted over the network.

## 4. Information sharing

Nexttech does not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. The app integrates only with the following Google services for limited technical purposes: Google ML Kit (on-device document edge detection and text recognition, no upload), Google Play Billing (processes the optional Lifetime Unlock purchase), Google Play In-App Review (shows the rating dialog when you initiate it). All interactions comply with Google's data policies.

## 5. Your rights

Because data resides on your device, you have full control to: delete individual documents inside the app, wipe all data by uninstalling the app, export documents through the system share sheet, and revoke permissions (camera, storage) at any time in system settings. Contact contact@nextsmart.vn for any privacy-related request or data deletion enquiry.

## 6. Policy changes

This policy may be updated over time. Any material change will be announced via app updates on Google Play, and a new version will replace the old one at the canonical URL at least 30 days before taking effect.

## 7. Google Play Services & ML Kit compliance

Free Scan integrates Google ML Kit Document Scanner and Text Recognition to power edge detection and OCR directly on your device. All image and text processing happens locally — no data is sent to Google. The integration complies with Google Play Policy, ML Kit Terms of Service, and the Google API Services User Data Policy.

> **Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure**
>
> The use of raw or derived user data received from Google Play services and ML Kit will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.
