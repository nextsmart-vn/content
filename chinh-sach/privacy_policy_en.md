# Privacy Policy — Free Scan

**Effective date:** 14 May 2026
**Developed by:** Nexttech smart solutions company limited
**Website:** https://nextsmart.vn
**Contact:** support@nextsmart.vn

## 1. Principles

Free Scan is designed to work fully offline. We **do not require accounts**, **do not collect personal data**, and **do not upload your documents to any server**.

## 2. Data we process

| Data | Purpose | Stored where |
|---|---|---|
| Scanned document images | Display + export PDF/JPG | Your device |
| OCR text | Display + copy/share | Your device |
| App settings (language, theme) | Persist preferences | Your device |
| Scan counter (used for rating prompt) | UX | Your device |

We do **not** transmit any of this data anywhere.

## 3. Permissions

Free Scan requests:

- **Camera** (`CAMERA`): to capture documents. Required for scanning.
- **Storage** (`READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`): optional, to open existing images.
- **Internet** (`INTERNET`): used only for Google Play Billing (if you opt to buy Lifetime Unlock) and Google Play In-App Review. Never used to send your documents.

You can revoke any permission anytime in system settings.

## 4. Third parties

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Google ML Kit (Document Scanner + Text Recognition) | Edge detection, OCR | Runs fully on-device. No upload. |
| Google Play Billing | Handles Lifetime Unlock IAP | Only if user opts to purchase |
| Google Play In-App Review | Shows rating prompt | Only if user opts to rate |

## 5. In-app purchases

Free Scan has **one** optional IAP: **Lifetime Unlock** (99,000 VND, one-time).

- Effect: hides the single banner ad at the bottom of the document list
- Non-renewing
- We never collect your payment info — Google Play handles everything
- Restore via "Restore purchases" in Settings

## 6. Advertising

If you haven't purchased Lifetime Unlock, the app shows **one** banner ad at the bottom of the main document list. This banner:

- Does **not** appear in scan, editor, OCR, or export screens
- Does **not** interrupt scan workflows
- Does **not** show interstitial / app-open / native ads
- Can be removed permanently with Lifetime Unlock

Ads are served by Google AdMob. Google may collect ad interaction data per [Google AdMob policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 7. Children

Free Scan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Your rights

Because data lives on your device:
- Delete: uninstall the app or delete individual documents in the app
- Export: use the in-app Share feature
- Edit: use the Editor / OCR result screen

## 9. Policy changes

We will notify you via app update if this policy changes.
